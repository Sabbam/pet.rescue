First Aid

The user captures the photograph of the animal that is isolated/injured or needed to be taken care of and posts it into the application. The location details are updated on the photograph. The animal(dog/cat) is then directed to the nearest rescue center with the assistance of rescue team. In case of injury, the animal is headed to the center after the team provides minimal first aid to it.

×

Adoption

After the team safely moves the animal (cat/dog) to the rescue center, the same animal could be adopted by any user of the application. He/she will be directed to carry out the process with the help of location details of the center. The later process will be monitored by the center.

Save a Life

Rescuing animals is a noble act of saving creatures from danger or suffering. It can involve a variety of situations, from plucking a kitten from a tree to rehabilitating a wild animal injured in a car accident. Animal shelters and rescue organizations take in animals that have been abandoned, surrendered, or seized from abusive owners. These animals are then provided with food, shelter, medical care, and socialization until they can be adopted into loving homes.

×

Donation

Animal rescue centers provide temporary care for lost, abandoned, or abused animals. They work to find these animals loving homes, provide them with medical care, and give them the love and attention they deserve. Donations to animal rescue centers are essential to their work. Every rupee donated helps them to provide food, shelter, and medical care for the animals in their care. Your tiny contribution can fill the hearts of the poor creatures with tender happiness.
