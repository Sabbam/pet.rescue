About Us

Very often we come across animals that are in the baby stage and are isolated in bitter weather conditions. We also find those that are injured and strive to find a shelter. In this process, we always think that we need to provide an aid to them but do not know how to initiate. We are providing aid to those dejected animals by directing them to rescue centers in case of injury and adoption procedure in case they are found isolated in an intolerant environment.
